Tacos

Description

Easy and flavorful. You will love these tacos! You can use top sirloin, New York, filet mignon or similar cuts of beef.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Lime
  • Spice
  • Shell

Steps

  1. Season the steak.
  2. Make the salsa.
  3. Heat the meat and shell.
  4. Add meat and salsa to shell.
